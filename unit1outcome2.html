Improving productivity with IT
Unit 1 Outcome 2



2.1 Review the ongoing use of IT tools and change approach as needed – in making your own website, are you happy with the tools you used? E.g. Visual Studio Code, Trello

For my website, I have used Trello for planning- it has allowed me to prepare all the steps and some direction on how to complete the tasks. I have originally used a we design template but ended up changing to a simple Kanban template with To do, Doing and done. This allowed me to reduce many steps that were not relevant for this project and the change saved me time.

I have used Visual Studio Code (VS Code)throughout and the flexibility and user-friendly tasks allowed me to carry on with the code and also uploading the colours palette straight from Coolors.
Using Coolors allowed me to search and match the colours for my website in a very straight forward way. I found it to be user friendly and practical for the required task.

I have also used Draw.io for the website diagrams, this tool allowed me to have a clear visual prompt on the steps and project. Draw.io, has taken me a few sessions to learn. But once I understood the processes and how the layout expands according to the needs of the project, it gave me a different perspective of the potential. I now use it for most of the planning and as concrete visual tool.

2.2 Describe whether the IT tools selected were appropriate E.g. Visual Studio Code, Trello

For this project, I have used Trello, Draw.io, VS Code, Canva and Coolors. I have found all the tools appropriate and easy to use for this project.

2.3 Assess the strengths and weaknesses of your final project – are you happy with your website? Why? Why not? You should review it on the website itself in the interests of openness. What would you change?

I am happy with the Webpage as it was my first time completing this type of project. I like the clean look and feel of the page.

In terms of functionality and aesthetics it does need further work and improvements, I could have added a logo, more images and worked on the accessibility tools. However, I'm aware that I do not possess the technical skills and knowledge required to complete the tasks the way I have envisaged.

2.4 Describe further improvements you can make to your project

I would have added the following improvements:

Navigation bar with separated buttons, with symbols and aligned to the right side.
2 column layout, with the second column for a search bar on interesting blog subjects.
Use less words for more impact and add videos, audio and more accessibility tools.

2.5 Review outcomes to make sure they match requirements and are fit for purpose

All outcomes were reviewd and meet the criteria! ✅
