3.2 Describe ways to improve productivity and efficiency

Ways to improve productivity can include;

clarity with the task ahead, good and effective planning, delegation of tasks, team members responsibility and accountability, tracking progress at various stages.

During the project, I found using Trello and the checklist in a systematic way allowed me to reduce the stress levels and worries about technical aspects of the project and increased my creativity and ideas.

Using Slack, as a tool to improve productivity.

In addition, having clarity, tracking the project, remove distractions and also important its wellbeing , and good time management.

The focus should be:

Personal: accountability, responsibility and "mind" your health and wellbeing. ✅

Technology or external factors: Goals, lists, time management and tracking. ✅



3.3 Develop solutions to improve own productivity using IT in digital projects

For my next project, I would definitely implement more digital productivity tools such as slack and Trello. I would have the team clearly dividing tasks and ensuring accountability for their work and learning. Part of team-work is to also ensure support is available and members are not overwhelmed with the tasks.

Im still designing my list of favourite tools to use. Currently starts with:

Step 1 - clarity on tasks, goals and objectives and good planning.

Step 2 - Set up Slack and Trello.

Step 3 - Draw.io and flow charts. Reviews and adapt as needed.

Step 4 - Have a working and flexible system, checklist for tracking, review, adapt and test!
